About the Game

It's all about moving to the music

It's basically an endless runner, but the twist is everything syncs up with whatever track you're playing. Instead of simply avoiding obstacles, players move through environments that transform according to the soundtrack. When it clicks, you'll get into this zone where you're not even thinking - just flowing with the music.
